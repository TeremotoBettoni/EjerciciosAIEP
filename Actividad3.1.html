OUR DESIGNS

Decorated biscuits are a popular treat that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. These small, sweet treats are typically made from a simple combination of flour, butter, sugar and eggs, which are then shaped into different shapes using cookie cutters before being baked until golden brown. What makes decorated biscuits different from ordinary biscuits are the fun and creative designs that decorate their surfaces. Some decorated biscuits are simply sprinkled with powdered sugar, while others are covered in colourful icing or decorated with intricate designs in edible fondant or royal icing. Whether enjoyed as a special treat or given as a thoughtful gift, decorated biscuits are a delicious way to add a touch of whimsy and charm to any occasion.
